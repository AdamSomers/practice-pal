# v0.1.1 — April 6, 2026

## Bug Fixes
- **Session persistence**: Sessions stored in PostgreSQL instead of server memory. No more random logouts on staging when requests hit different machines or after deploys.
- **Practice session resume**: Refreshing the page mid-practice now resumes the session with all checkoffs intact instead of starting a new one.
- **401 redirect softened**: Session expiry during a save no longer kicks you to login and loses your work. Error surfaces instead, giving you a chance to copy your work.

## Improvements
- **Chart builder draft persistence**: Unsaved chart builder state auto-saves to localStorage. Survives page refresh and crashes.
- **Leave confirmation**: Navigating away from chart builder with unsaved items prompts to Save, Discard, or Keep editing. Browser refresh/close triggers native "Leave page?" prompt.
